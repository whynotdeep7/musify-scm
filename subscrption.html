Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? +
You'll get 1 month of Musify Premium for free when you sign up. After the trial period ends, your subscription will automatically continue at the standard monthly rate unless you cancel before the trial ends.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. If you cancel during your free trial, you won't be charged.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.
Can I switch plans later? +
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes will take effect at your next billing cycle.
